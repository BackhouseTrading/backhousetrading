About the Program

Built to capitalize on
market dislocations

The Discretionary Macro Program combines top-down macroeconomic analysis, positioning intelligence, and observational price action to identify asymmetric opportunities across global futures markets.

Rules-Based Risk Management

Every trade is sized by stop-loss distance and capital-at-risk—protecting capital systematically while preserving upside participation.

Low Correlation to Traditional Assets

Historically negative correlation to the S&P 500, providing genuine portfolio diversification.

Liquid, Transparent Structure

Futures-only portfolio constrained to highly liquid U.S. contracts. Managed account structure with full transparency and daily liquidity.

Tax-Efficient Vehicle

Futures contracts receive 60/40 long-term/short-term capital gains treatment under IRC Section 1256, improving after-tax returns.
